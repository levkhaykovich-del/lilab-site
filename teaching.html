Courses taught by Prof. Lev Khaykovich

Courses

Current Teaching

Undergraduate

Physics 3 — Optics & Modern Physics

Introduction to wave optics, interference, diffraction, and the foundations of modern physics: special relativity, quantum mechanics, atomic structure, and nuclear physics.

Graduate

Atomic Physics

Advanced treatment of atomic structure, light-matter interaction, laser physics, laser cooling and trapping, Bose–Einstein condensation, and selected topics in ultracold quantum gases.

Graduate

Orientation in Physics — Time

Interdisciplinary exploration of the concept of time in physics — from classical mechanics and thermodynamics to quantum theory and relativity.
